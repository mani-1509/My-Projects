    WHAT
    IS CALLED ART?

    Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consciously created through an expression of skill or imagination.

    7 TYPES OF ART

    The seven different art forms are Painting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music . However, back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the Liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic, Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.

    About Us

    Artezza is a vibrant platform dedicated to artists of all levels, where creativity and community come together. Whether you're a budding painter or an experienced artist, Artezza provides a space for you to showcase your artwork, share your skills, and connect with fellow creators. With a focus on quality materials and innovative tools, Artezza empowers artists to express themselves fully. The platform encourages collaboration and inspiration, offering features that allow users to post their paintings, receive feedback, and participate in challenges. Join Artezza to celebrate your artistic journey and inspire others with your unique vision!
